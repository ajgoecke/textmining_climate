Thomas Paintner joined IKEM as a Research Associate in January 2021. His main field of activity is energy law with a focus on alternative fuels. In the Campfire project, he is researching the legal framework for the establishment and operation of service stations to supply ships with fuel based on green ammonia.
He studied law at the University of Regensburg and the University of Aberdeen with a focus on European and international law. After his first law degree, he completed a Master of Laws (LL.M.) at the Universiteit van Amsterdam in the field of international and European law.
