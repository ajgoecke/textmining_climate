CV
Timon Plass is a Research Associate at IKEM since January 2021. During the summer 2020 he already worked three months at the IKEM during his Legal Traineeship and assisted various projects in the Mobility Team and the Energy Transition in Transport Team.
Timon Plass studied Law at the Alberts-Ludwigs-University Freiburg and the National and Kapodistrian University Athens with a focus on intellectual property law. He conducted his Legal Traineeship at Landgericht Chemnitz which included a stage at the Legal Directorate of the MDR Leipzig and at a law office specialized in family law.
