Biography
Hermann Blümel is a Senior Research Associate at IKEM, responsible for the cross-cutting topics of traffic/mobility, energy, climate and environment.
He studied mechanical engineering with a focus on power and working machines as well as automotive engineering. Since 1980 he has been working on the integration of transport planning/mobility, environmental and climate protection in transport and transport technology as well as their political and regulatory implementation. After his studies, he worked in the automotive industry as a development engineer in the field of fuel consumption and exhaust gases, then at the Federal Environment Agency, the Federal Ministry for the Environment, as an independent consultant and in various Senate departments of the State of Berlin. He has many years of experience in the development of EU and national regulation, has led several projects funded by the State of Berlin, the Federal Government and the EU and was a member of working groups of the ECE, the EU and in European city networks.
