Benjamin Grosse works as a senior research associate at IKEM since January 2021, he is a member of our Mobility team. His work focuses on sector coupling and business model analysis. As an industrial engineer, he supports various projects at the interface between technology, regulation and economic analysis.
He studied Bachelor Energy and Environmental Management at Flensburg University of Applied Sciences and spent one semester at Al Akhawayn University in Morocco. He then transferred to TU Berlin, where he completed a master’s degree in industrial engineering with a focus on energy and resource management. After his graduation, he worked for six years as a research assistant at the FG Energy and Resource Management of the TU Berlin. In addition to his employment at IKEM, he is an active lecturer and member of the Working Group on Renewable Energies and Energy Technology of the VDI Berlin-Brandenburg.
