Energy Transition Coloring Book wins European Design Award
The Infographic Energy Transition Coloring Book was awarded gold at the European Design Awards in Oslo this Saturday. The European Design Awards celebrate excellence and originality in European design, with expert judges from each European country selecting the winners.
The Infographic Energy Transition Coloring Book is the result of a collaboration between IKEM’s research and story telling and the talented designers and illustrators of Ellery Studio for Creative Design, and took home Gold in the category Infographics (print). The visualisations and infographics of the book lay out the big picture of climate change, the achievements and challenges of the German energy transition and the role of the individual in turning things around. The book is the first in a new series of communication tools designed to inform and engage everyone, from policy-makers to industry to individuals, into the shift to renewable energy sources and sustainable lifestyles.
The European Design Award ist already the second high-profile award for the Infographic Energy Transition Coloring Book: In March it was awarded at the Malofiej Awards.

In Zusammenarbeit mit den Designern von Ellery Studio entstand das erste Malbuch zur Energiewende. Das Buch verpackt das komplexe Thema Energie- und Klimaschutzpolitik in einer Reihe von leicht verständlichen und spielerischen Infografiken, die nur darauf warten mit Farbe gefüllt zu werden!
English: IKEM teamed up with the design agency Ellery Studio to develop the first-of-its-kind Infographic Energy Transition Coloring Book! The book tells the story of climate change and the energy transition in Germany in an accessible and lighthearted way, packed in a series of playful infographics to be colored in by you! In addition to providing a fun and meditative activity, this book also teaches you actionable steps to cool down our planet and live sustainably. From the dynamics of solar panels to the nitty gritty of the Paris Agreement, the book chock-full of factoids and helpful tips.
