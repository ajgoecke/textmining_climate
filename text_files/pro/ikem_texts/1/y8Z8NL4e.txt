Within Move Urban IKEM is conducting research into the legal framework for space-saving, integrated neighborhood mobility services and possible operator models for a new urban quarter in Berlin
In the LaWoMa project, IKEM supports the development of a local energy supply concept for the Mariendorf residential park of Gewobag in Berlin-Mariendorf.
IKEM supports the expansion of Berlin's charging point network. This project aims to improve access to charging points, especially for people without private parking spaces.
IKEM is developing strategies for the city of Bottrop to ensure the competitiveness of the regional economy during the transition to electromobility.
The project USER-CHI aims to promote transnational and inter-regional electric mobility in Europe with IKEM focussing on data management and ethical issues.
