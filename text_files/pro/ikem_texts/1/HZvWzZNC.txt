CV
Jana Karras is a PhD student of University of Greifswald and a former intern at IKEM, now supporting IKEM as a research assistant with a focus on energy efficiency and climate finance.
Jana Karras has a bachelor’s and master’s degree in law from the National University “Law Academy of Ukraine of Yaroslav the Wise” with a focus on international public law. She then studied law at the University of Greifswald and successfully passed the first state examination in March 2021. The focus of her studies was on public law and comparative law.
Jana Karras is writing her PhD thesis on the topic “Legal barriers of thermal efficiency improvement in dwellings populated by low-income tenants and opportunities to overcome them. Case study of Germany” under the supervision of Prof. Dr. Rodi at the University of Greifswald
