Yasin Yilmaz is a Senior Research Associate at the Institute for Climate Protection, Energy and Mobility. He works in the energy law team and deals primarily with fiscal and tax-based matters of the energy transition.
Mr. Yilmaz studied law at the Free University of Berlin and focused on economic, corporate and tax law. He completed his legal clerkship at the Berlin Court of Appeal from 2016 to 2018. His clerkship included stations at the Federal Ministry for Economic Affairs and Energy, the law firm Dentons and the German-Turkish Chamber of Commerce and Industry in Istanbul.
He is doing his doctorate on carbon pricing under the supervision of Prof. Dr. Rodi at the University of Greifswald.
