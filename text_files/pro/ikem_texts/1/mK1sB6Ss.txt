CV
Rimante Balsiunaite is a last year student of Erasmus Mundus Joint Master’s Degree in Environmental Sciences, Policy and Management, now supporting IKEM as an intern focusing on energy efficiency and climate finance.
Rimante holds a bachelor’s degree in communications from Vilnius Tech University with a focus on intercultural communications. During her communication studies, she was an exchange student at Stuttgart Media University (Germany) and Xi’an Jiaotong University (China). After studying, Rimante started working as an environmental communications specialist.
Rimante writes her MSc thesis on clean energy innovation financing in Lithuania at Central European University.
