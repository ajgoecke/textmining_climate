Prof. Udo Onnen-Weber was born in 1948 in Aurich, East Frisia. He studied education at the University of Kiel and subsequently architecture at the University of Hannover. He then worked as a freelance architect before becoming Professor of Design and CAD at the Faculty of Architecture at Wismar University in 1993. In 2007 he founded the Competence Centre for Rural Mobility, Wismar University of Applied Sciences, of which he has been the director ever since. He also heads the project management centre of the electro-mobility region Mecklenburg-Vorpommern for the BMVI.
