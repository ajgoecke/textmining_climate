CV
Friedemann Kallmeyer is a research associate at IKEM in the field of mobility.
He supports the team, among other things, in the implementation of research projects for the automation of traffic, for example Hamburg Electric Autonomous Transportation (HEAT). His topics include the integration of automated and autonomous minibuses, new mobility offers and concepts, infrastructure and vehicle technology in connection with automated vehicles.
He is studying transportation engineering at the Technical University of Dresden and specializes in transportation planning and technology with a focus on vehicle automation, especially in connection with local public transportation. At the end of his studies, Friedemann Kallmeyer is doing research on the effects of the integration of new mobility offers in traffic systems. He is currently writing his final thesis on scenarios and the evaluation of autonomous bus transport services.
