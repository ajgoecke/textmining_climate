Biography
Adrian Röhrig supports the IKEM as a research associate in public and media relations.
He completed his Bachelor’s degree in Political- and Communications Science at the Friedrich-Schiller-Universität Jena and his Master’s degree at the Freie Universität Berlin. As part of his Erasmus he studied for half a year at the Norges teknisk-naturvitenskapelige universitet Trondheim in Norway.
During his studies he worked as a student assistant in the Department of Political Science of the FSU Jena and as student assistant in public and media relations for the German Pharmaceutical Industry Association (BPI).
