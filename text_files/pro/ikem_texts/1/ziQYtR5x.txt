IKEM is a partner of the Interdisciplinary Research Center for the Baltic Sea Region (IFZO) based at the University of Greifswald. In IFZO's research area Energy, IKEM analyzes the energy transition in the Baltic Sea region from a legal and socio-political perspective.
The Kopernikus project Ariadne aims to provide decision-makers with an improved scientific basis for shaping the German energy system transformation through an evidence-based assessment.
The "Netz-Stabil" research network is investigating how a stable and reliable energy supply can be achieved despite fluctuating generation.
