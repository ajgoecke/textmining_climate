Biography
Odile is a Graphic Designer at IKEM. She is in charge of translating elaborate research into visually compelling infographics and posters. She ensures that the IKEM design guidelines are consistent in online and printed publications.
Odile studied Industrial Design at Brunel University in London, specialising in service design and communication.
