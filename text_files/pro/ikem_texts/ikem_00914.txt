Biography
Sandra Jankowski has been a team assistant at IKEM since April 2018 and supports the secretariat of the managing director.
Sandra Jankowski studied social and cultural anthropology and Portuguese-Brazilian studies at the Freie Universität Berlin and the Universidade de Coimbra, Portugal. During her studies in Coimbra she supported the administration of the local theatre company at the Teatro da Cerca de São Bernardo.
After graduating, she worked as a secretary at a private acting school in Berlin until she started her work at IKEM.
