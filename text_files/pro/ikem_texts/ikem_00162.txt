IKEM is the speaker for the third field, „Hydrogen in administrative directives, authorisation practice and programmes“, whose goal is the analysis of the relevant regulatory aspects and the issuing of proposals for regulatory amendments. The activity field itself is divided into four working groups: administrative directives (public procurement of hydrogen vehicles and other hydrogen-based technologies), authorisation practice (permitting for projects involving hydrogen-based technologies), technical standards and funding programmes.
