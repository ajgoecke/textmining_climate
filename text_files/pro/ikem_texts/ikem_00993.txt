Biography
Franziska Holzgrebe works in the Accounting/Controlling department at IKEM since June 2019.
Franziska Holzgrebe studied business administration with a focus on taxation and auditing at Hochschule für Wirtschaft und Recht Berlin. During her studies and also after graduation, she worked as a tax assistant in a medium-sized tax consulting firm until September 2014. Before taking up her position at IKEM, Franziska Holzgrebe worked as a tax and audit assistant for Becker Büttner Held PartGmbB/Invra Treuhand AG.
