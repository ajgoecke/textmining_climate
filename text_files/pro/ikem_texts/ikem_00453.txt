Biografie
He is Deputy Chairman of GEODE (European Association of Independent Electricity and Gas Distributors/Dealers), Chairman of the AEEC (Associated European Energy Consultants) and Chairman of the Supervisory Board of inVra Treuhand AG Wirtschaftsprüfungsgesellschaft. Christian Held is a co-publisher of the journal “InfrastrukturRecht” (CH Beck-Verlag) as well as managing partner of the Weingut Klostermühle Odernheim/Nahe In recognition of his long-term commitment to science and teaching at TH Bingen, Christian Held was appointed an honorary professor in 2017.
At the Institute for Climate Protection, Energy and Mobility, he holds the position of Deputy Chairman.
Christian Held conducts extensive editorial and lecturing activities related to energy law.
