Biography
Anne Freiberger is a Research Associate at IKEM and works on topics in the field of electromobility and digitalisation.
She studied Law at the Free University of Berlin, the Istanbul University and received a Master of Laws (LL.M.) in Global Environment and Climate Change Law at the University of Edinburgh. She conducted her Legal Traineeship at Kammergericht Berlin, including a stage at the Federal Ministry for the Environment, Nature Conservation and Nuclear Safety. She gained further professional experience at the Administration of the German Federal Parliament (Parlamentary Advisory Council on Sustainable Development) and adelphi (Policy advice).
