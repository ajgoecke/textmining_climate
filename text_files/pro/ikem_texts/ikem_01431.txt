Aleksandra Novikova is among the training staff of the ECRAN project on quantitative models for the assessment of climate and energy policies in the Western Balkans. This project is financed by the EU Technical Assistance and Information Exchange (TAIEX) in the framework of the Environment and Climate Regional Accession Network (ECRAN). It aims to builds capacity in the countries of the Western Balkans to allow countries to use quantitative models and scenario development for the assessment of climate and energy targets and policy options. The beneficiaries of the training are the Ministries of Environment in Albania, Bosnia and Herzegovina, Croatia, the Former Yugoslav Republic of Macedonia, Iceland, Kosovo, Montenegro, Serbia and Turkey. The project was implemented until the end of 2015.
