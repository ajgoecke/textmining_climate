Prof. Dr. Michael Rodi is the Director and Academic Advisor of IKEM. He has served as the Chair of Public Law, Financial Law, Environmental and Energy Law at the Faculty of Law and Political Science of the University of Greifswald since 1999. His research focuses on climate change policy, sustainable energy management and transport, and tax and subsidy law.
Prof. Dr. Rodi coordinates IKEM’s interdisciplinary research and is the dissertation advisor for several young scientists. As Chairman of the IKEM Board, he plays a key role in defining the Institute’s strategic orientation and focus.
