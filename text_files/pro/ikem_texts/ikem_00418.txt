Biography
Ulrike Hertel works as project controller in the finance department at IKEM since June 2020.
After her graduation in business administration at the Berufsakademie Glauchau and a year abroad in France she worked as project controller in an IT-company in Potsdam. Before starting at IKEM, Ulrike Hertel worked as accountant and controller in the finance department of a language school in Berlin.
