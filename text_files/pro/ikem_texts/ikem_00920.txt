Biography
David Stegmaier works as a Research Associate for the mobility team at IKEM in Berlin. He focuses on autonomous driving, autonomous systems and AI.
He studied law at Viadrina University of Frankfurt (Oder) with a focus on private and business law. In addition to that, he studied Italian Law at Humboldt-University of Berlin. He completed his legal clerkship in Berlin, during which he worked for the city of Berlin’s office for urban development, a lawyer specialized in immigration law and IKEM’s energy team.
