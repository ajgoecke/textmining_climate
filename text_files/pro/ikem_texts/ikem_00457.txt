The IKEM Annual Report 2017, which was published today in Berlin looks back on a successful year: With numerous new national and international research projects, the institute was able to expand its research activities even further. The IKEM team has grown as well: Today around 40 experts are working in Greifswald, Berlin, Stuttgart and Brussels to promote sustainable mobility, renewable energies and climate protection.
The 27-page annual report highlights the diverse work of IKEM in the past year. In addition to the constant expansion of the national and international project activities, last year’s focus was on strenghtening IKEM’s visibility as an NGO and on making the IKEM events more professional. This includes in particular the IKEM Annual Conference and the IKEM Summer Academy.
The IKEM Annual Report is now available for download (only available in German).
IKEM’s work is supported by numerous donors, sponsors and strategic partners. By making a donation to IKEM, you too have the opportunity to support the institute’s research. Get in touch with us for more information.
