CV
Victoria Harsch joined IKEM’s energy law team as a Research Assistant in May 2020.
She studied law at the University of Constance and the University of Oxford. While studying, she focused on environment, public planning and public commercial law. She completed the legal clerkship in Berlin during which she worked at the Federal Ministry of Education and Research and at the law firm Posser Spieth Wolfers & Partners located in Berlin as well as at IKEM’s energy law team. She gained further professional experience at the law firm SammlerUsinger mbB in Berlin.
