CV
Lukas Benner is a research assistant at IKEM since January 2021, focusing on energy law.
He studied law at the Rheinische Friedrich-Wilhelms-Universität Bonn with a focus on European and international law of economic relations.
He is doing his PhD thesis on “Decarbonization of Shipping- Opportunities and Challenges in the Area of Conflict between the Nation State, the European Union and the International Maritime Organization” under the supervision of Prof. Dr. Rodi at the University of Greifswald.
